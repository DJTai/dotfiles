# dotfiles

Here are some of my dotfiles
- **OS:** Manjaro Linux
- **WM:** i3
- **Shell:** bash
- **Terminal:** urxvt

**NOTE**: Copy + paste at your own risk. I highly recommend that you use my files as a reference, i.e., don't just replace your file with mine. Please backup your files before you attempt to change anything on your computer.

### Where are these files located?
- All of the files and folders that begin with a `.`, e.g., `.i3` or `.bashrc`, are typically found in your home direcory. For example, if your username on your computer is XYZ, then these files would go into `/home/XYZ`
- The `etc` folder is found at `/etc`
